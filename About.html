About Me

I am a graduate of Bells University, where I studied Mechatronics Engineering. Currently, I am a student of the ALX Software Engineering program, and I am passionate about developing innovative software solutions. I would like to leverage my programming knowledge to create software solutions for robotic system development. With strong proficiency in Python, C, SQL, JavaScript, HTML, and React, I specialize in creating efficient, scalable, and high-quality applications. I have hands-on experience in projects like developing a chess engine and am dedicated to continuous learning and improvement in the field of software engineering. In addition to my software projects, I have worked on several robotics projects, including a wireless hand gesture-controlled robotic arm and a smart self-navigation land rover utilizing various sensors. These experiences have further fueled my passion for integrating software development with robotics to create cutting-edge solutions.
